Merge AU

Preface

Merge AU takes place inside a world not unlike Earth, where all main characters in Baba Is You are genuses.
All of these coexist, and there is a similar society to modern day.
This is not post-scarcity, and why that is will be explained later.

Story

Jiji

Our story starts with Jiji.

Nameless by choice, going only by Jiji, they started out living on the streets.
Being born intersex at a hatefully exclusionary time and place, they were outcast from their potential life.
Their parents kicked them out to the streets at a very, very young age, and this left an impression on them.
They were gifted with their extreme charm and universal expertise, and if not for this, may have changed the world forever.
They started out in their preteen years already high-ranking among local gangs,
and by the time they started to mature, they were already a well-known and feared criminal.

Around that time, they discovered their affinity for TEXT and manipulation of it.
In using these skills, they turned themself from the local mafia’s leader to a world-feared villain.
They committed countless atrocities, including arson, xxxx xxxx, torture, and massacre, to name a few.

Eventually, Jiji grew tired of their leadership position, and moved on to their own ideas.
Working alone out of insanity, they grew to understand how TEXT was created, and how to make their own.
One of their first and only experiments with this custom text was MERGE, which is where Balt comes into play.

Balt

Balt was created via the merging of a Baba and a Belt.
Due to a fault in Jiji’s programming of MERGE, however, this was incorrectly handled.
Instead of keeping the soul of the Baba and transforming it, the existing soul was expunged into the afterlife immediately,
and a new, similar soul was created in its place, without more than memory of what the world is and the essentials to live in it.
Balt immediately ran from Jiji’s workshop, but without anywhere to go, returned less than a quarter hour later.
Jiji, however, was massively affected by this, as the shockwave from the creation of this new soul had altered theirs as well.
After a few hours, Jiji woke up, and realized the horror of what they had done, and only wanted to atone.
They gave Balt a few lessons on the world, a sum of money to start their life, and went to turn themselves in.

Judgement

Jiji had set something up so that they could physically not be killed, so the local government compromised with them,
meaning Jiji lived out their entire sentence under a purgatorial medically-induced dream state.
Jiji’s full sentence was around 250 millenia after tallying all known crimes up, leaving Jiji in a dream state for 7 years.
In these 7 years, Balt started out a new life, making friends, enemies, living their life to the fullest.
He started out working odd jobs to pay the rent, but eventually got a career in biosimulation and its applications for virtual reality.
Balt made massive progress in the field, pioneering the SR (simulated reality) genre of games along with his team.

Amalgam

After their release, Jiji was extremely shaken up, as they had lost most of their memory,
and only had what they had written down beforehand to go off of.
They sought out Balt, and he caught them up to speed on what had changed, and what he knew they knew.
Using this knowledge, Jiji started out with their experiments again (under governmental eye),
and isolated a serum that could temporarily merge two objects without TEXT, and if applicable, while keeping their souls intact.
After a few isolated experiments, Jiji decided to ask around for volunteers to create an amalgam and run tests on its stability.
During this testing, however, Balt fell in.

Without proper preparation beforehand, Balt was instantly killed, and his body amalgamated.
Normally, most people are forced into the afterlife after only a few minutes,
and most don’t have time to learn how to interact with the world as a ghost, but Balt was an exception.
Due to Jiji’s improper handling of merge years prior, he isn’t able to move on to the afterlife yet.
Since his soul was created artificially and without an ID, he can’t move on until everyone else has -
that is, he has to wait for armageddon.
After being calmed from a panic attack by Balt, Jiji aborted the experiment early and sent everyone home.
Balt decided not to press charges, as it was a tragic accident caused by his mistake.
However, after a bit of learning, he could still work, and talk, and everything else as a ghost,
making it almost, almost as if he had never died.

Post-death

After getting back on his feet, keeping his job, Balt decided he was sick of not having a body.
Working with Jiji for the more strenuous parts,
Balt created a mechanical, soul-driven body to inhabit, with very similar looks to his old one.
Made from metal and rubber, Balt enjoyed being in this body, as it made him feel secure,
but he felt there was some things he could not do.
After a few months of working alone (Jiji had their own things to do), Balt had created a few more bodies:

  • One to swim effectively, allowing him to enjoy the sea
  • One to fly himself at high speed through the air (given FCC clearance, of course)
  • One to withstandthe hottest of temperatures, able to sit in hot lava and be fine

Balt no longer had to pay for groceries or water bills, as he no longer needed them, which set him ahead in finances.
This let him collect his income much more effectively, and live a little large.
However, he realized that he hates living lavishly, and would rather stay small,
so he picked up and moved to an apartment-sized house near Jiji’s.
They visit each other often.

Present Day

Nowadays, everybody in town knows or knows of Balt, and he rarely goes out inside a body anymore.
He works in software development, making a six-figure salary, and is very generous towards the community around him.
Legally, he's still considered as "alive", even though he isn't.
He's thinking of auctioning off or giving away the bodies that were made,
as he hasn't used any of them in years, opting instead to float around.

Notes

  • Most meat is obtained via postmortem donation, and is heavily regulated
    • Cannibalism is easily doable and hesitantly accepted, but prion diseases are a concern
    • If unfit meat passes inspection and poisons someone, the agency is at fault
  • The metric system is used on measurements below for convenience, although they use their own metric
    • Their metric is based solely on universal constants
      (e.g. the Planck length, the period of a hydrogen atom, etc)
  • All species have some form of telekinesis, which can be exercised to grow stronger
  • Some species can crossbreed, but the resulting child is very likely infertile
  • Words have a maximum radius, on average this is around 30 meters but text upward of a few kilometers has been found before
  • Words are extremely illegal to have unless you have a special goverment contract
  • It takes an unknown form of energy to create a rule, if a rule doesn’t have enough then it will not parse
    • The more severe the impact of the rule, the more of this energy is required
    • This is coloquially called entropy, though it’s likely not the same thing
    • This is why the world isn’t post-scarcity (it’s close though)

Genuses

Quadrupeds

Baba

  • .7 meters tall on average, +/- ~.2m
  • About the stature of a small boar, with white, extremely short fur
    • Lives best around mid-way between equator and poles
  • Coloration heavily dependent on species
  • Lives to be about 73 on average, +/- ~30 years
    • Biological maturity completes at ~19 years old

Jiji

  • Only a few species
  • 2 meters tall on average, +/- .5m
  • Horse-like, with fuzzy fur
    • Lives best near the poles
  • Shade can vary from red to yellow based on genetics, most are a shade of orange
    • Rarely, Jijis can be born with patterns
  • Body is about .7 meters in diameter
  • Lives to be about 45 on average, +/- ~8 years
    • Biological maturity completes at ~7 years old
  • Most common source of meat postmortem

Bipeds

Keke

  • 1.3 meters tall on average, +/- ~.1m
  • Different shades of red, most are mid-range
  • Fuzzy
    • Lives best around mid-way between equator and poles
  • Lives to around 97 on average, +/- ~50 years
    • Biological maturity completes at ~25 years old
  • Close common ancestor with Me

Me

  • 1.2 meters tall on average, +/- ~.1m
  • Color can vary from deep indigo to a dim magenta
  • Has skin, no hair
    • Lives best near the equator
  • Lives to be around 68 on average, +/- 4 years
    • Biological maturity completes at ~15 years old
  • Close common ancestor with Keke

Fofo

  • Thousands of different species
  • Color varies heavily between species
  • Plant-based, leaves and possible bearing fruit varies
    • Some fofos bear fruit, some bear flowers, some don’t bear anything
  • Only certain species can cross with certain others
  • Fofos grow in the ground, and dig out their own roots after about a year, after which memories and higher thought start
  • Life expectancy varies massively
    • Most common species live to be around ~600 years old on average, +/- 250 years
    • Biologically mature as soon as unrooted
  • Selling borne fruit/flowers alone is not profitable, as it’s too slow
    • Most vendors are large families, where there’s more to go around

Happenings

Sunbad

During governmental testing of a fixed MERGE, a space mission was launched to see the limit of the word.
A shuttle was shot off into deep space, eventually making it to one of the nearby solar systems.
A Badbad, hired to help, tried to throw a testing object into the system’s sun to merge it with,
but her momentum carried her into it instead.
Saved from a horrible fate, however, she merged with the sun.
After a few moments of talking, the mission ended as a failure, but with much more interesting testing to do.
Sunbad now watches over the few terraformed planets around her.

  • Due to her oblong shape, the planet now has eight different seasons and orbits in an oblong manner.
  • She can target solar flares to smite people, and has in the past killed corrupt leaders
  • She can talk, but rarely does because it wakes up everyone and makes people mad at her
    • There is one exception: New Year’s Eve/Day, she counts down to when it’s exactly inbetween her eyes
  • She’s going to stay alive until the star would’ve went supernova

Fbufbu

Fbufbu was an ancient species, seeming to be in the middle of the food chain.
A research team successfully birthed one from a lab, and they live in a city now.
They aren’t the brightest, but are sapient and a good person.

  • Fluffy with large bulbs of fur
  • Colored a bright cyan
  • Has four eyes in a square pattern
  • Has two ears
  • Has one front leg and two back legs
  • About as tall as a fofo
  • Herbivore
  • Very light
