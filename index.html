My payment timeline is as follows:

Timeline
  1. You send 40% payment up front
  2. I send a watermarked sketch
  3. You send 20% payment (or 60% for only sketch, stopping here and sending unwatermarked sketch)
  4. I send a watermarked unfinished version (probably flats / linework)
  5. You give feedback
  6. Repeat above 2 until you're happy with the artwork
  7. You send the remaining 40% payment
  8. I send the finished, unwatermarked piece, along with the project file
